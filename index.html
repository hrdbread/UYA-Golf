About us

Helping our children grown

The Ukrainian Youth Association (UYA) of Mississauga has been a long time contributor to the Credit Valley Hospital Foundation. It is currently generating money through the annual golf tournament for the funding of a second quiet room in this new expansion.

The UYA tournament funds are given to help provide a nurturing environment. This helps children grow. Not only grow physically but also mentally and spiritually. The funds allow the hospital to continue to give care to the children with cancer who need it for their wellness.
